Features of Apples....!

Apple fruit is oval or pear in shape.
They are incredibly nutritious fruit.
They are rich in fibre and antioxidants.
Its outer peel comes in different hues and colors depending upon the cultivar type.
Internally, its crispy, juicy pulp is off-white to cream and features a mix of mildly sweet and tart flavour.
Its seeds are bitter in taste, and therefore, inedible.
